MARPOL Annex I – Oily Water Discharge

ALLOWED:
- Oil content < 15 ppm
- Vessel en route
- Approved OWS and OCM operational
- Outside special areas

NOT ALLOWED:
- Discharge in special areas unless strict additional conditions are met
- If OWS or OCM malfunctioning
- If ppm readings unstable

KEY RISKS:
- Incorrect OCM calibration
- Misidentifying special area boundaries
- Incorrect valve lineup

---

MARPOL Annex V – Garbage Disposal

ALLOWED:
- Food waste:
  - >12 nm if untreated
  - >3 nm if comminuted or ground

NOT ALLOWED:
- Plastics NEVER to sea
- Mixed waste containing plastics
- Discharge in special areas (strict limitations)

KEY RISKS:
- Poor segregation onboard
- Crew misunderstanding discharge distances
- Incorrect Garbage Record Book entries

---

MARPOL Annex VI – Air Emissions

REQUIREMENTS:
- Use compliant fuel sulphur content (global ≤0.50%)
- ECA areas require ≤0.10% sulphur
- Maintain fuel changeover procedures and logs

KEY RISKS:
- Incorrect fuel changeover timing
- Missing or incomplete records
- Non-compliant fuel use in ECAs

---

SOLAS – Fire Main System

REQUIREMENTS:
- Fire pumps must deliver required pressure
- Must supply at least two jets simultaneously

EMERGENCY FIRE PUMP:
- Independently driven
- Located outside main machinery space
- Must function if engine room is lost

KEY RISKS:
- Loss of main pump in fire
- Poor maintenance leading to pressure loss
